Banking Application

A Banking Application enables customers to manage bank accounts, transfer funds, pay bills, view transactions, and access financial services through a secure digital platform.

Core Modules

Module Description
Customer Management Manage customer accounts and profiles.
Account Management Handle savings, current, and loan accounts.
Transaction Processing Manage deposits, withdrawals, and transfers.
Bill Payments Support utility and service payments.
Security Authentication, authorization, and fraud protection.
