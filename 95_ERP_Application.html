Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Application

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) software integrates various business processes such as Finance, Human Resources, Inventory, Sales, Procurement, and Production into a single centralized system.

Core ERP Modules

Module Description
Human Resources Manage employees, payroll, and attendance.
Finance Handle accounting, budgeting, and transactions.
Inventory Track stock levels and warehouse operations.
Sales Manage orders, customers, and invoices.
Procurement Manage suppliers and purchasing activities.
Reporting Generate business analytics and reports.
