Healthcare Management System

A Healthcare Management System helps hospitals, clinics, and healthcare providers manage patients, appointments, doctors, medical records, billing, and healthcare services efficiently.

Core Modules

Module Description
Patient Management Maintain patient records and profiles.
Appointment Management Schedule and manage appointments.
Doctor Management Manage doctor schedules and information.
Medical Records Store diagnosis, prescriptions, and reports.
Billing Handle payments and invoices.

Key Features

  • Patient registration and management.
  • Online appointment scheduling.
  • Doctor and staff management.
  • Electronic medical records.
  • Prescription tracking.
  • Billing and payment processing.
  • Reports and analytics.
  • Role-based user access.

Benefits

  • Improves patient care and service quality.
  • Reduces paperwork and manual errors.
  • Centralizes healthcare information.
  • Enhances hospital efficiency.
  • Supports faster decision-making.
